Diabetes Data Visualization with Tableau

This project involves the visualization of diabetes data, focusing on key demographic and clinical factors. The analysis includes the age distribution of patients, highlighting how diabetes prevalence varies across different age groups. Additionally, it examines the gender distribution, providing insights into any disparities between male and female patients. The visualization also covers a range of common symptoms associated with diabetes, offering a comprehensive overview of the typical clinical presentations observed in diabetic individuals. This detailed visualization aids in understanding the demographics and symptoms prevalent among diabetes patients, contributing to better-informed healthcare strategies.
